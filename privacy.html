Privacy Policy

Newsletter Bot values your privacy. This policy outlines what limited data we collect, how it is used, and how your information is protected. By using the bot, you consent to the practices outlined below.

What We Collect

We only store the minimum data needed to deliver your personalized news updates:

  • Discord User ID – to securely associate your preferences with your account
  • News Preferences – your selected categories and delivery method (DM or channel)
  • Delivery Settings – including the time of day you wish to receive updates
  • Your Timezone – used to schedule delivery accurately (not auto-detected; you can choose any timezone for privacy)

Why We Store This

This data allows the bot to deliver relevant, timely, and customized content. Without it, we cannot provide scheduled updates or respect your preferences.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Any messages or content you send on Discord
  • Email addresses, IP addresses, or device information
  • Behavioral analytics or external account data

Third-Party Sharing

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party. It is used exclusively to provide the functionality of the bot.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is retained only as long as needed for active use. You may delete your data at any time by using the bot’s /unsubscribe or /reset command, or by contacting us directly.

Security

Data is stored using secure, industry-standard methods. Access is strictly limited and monitored to prevent unauthorized use or tampering.

Policy Updates

This policy may be revised as the bot evolves. We will notify users of significant changes via Discord announcements or the project website.
