Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Newsletter Bot. Your privacy matters to us. This document explains what limited data we collect and how we use it, with full transparency and respect for your digital rights.

What We Collect

Newsletter Bot only stores the minimum data required to deliver a personalized experience:

  • Discord User ID – used to associate your preferences with your account securely.
  • Your News Preferences – selected news categories and delivery method (DM or channel).
  • Delivery Settings – including your chosen delivery time.
  • Your Timezone – used to send news at the correct local time for you.

Why We Store This

This data allows us to provide scheduled, relevant, and user-specific news delivery. Without it, the bot wouldn’t be able to function as intended.

What We Don't Collect

We explicitly do not collect or store:

  • Messages or content you send in Discord
  • Your email address, IP address, or any external account information
  • Behavioral analytics or device/browser fingerprints

Third-Party Sharing

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. All information is used exclusively for bot functionality and remains stored securely.

Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your data at any time using the bot's opt-out or reset command. If you remove the bot from your server or revoke its access, all associated data will be deleted automatically within a reasonable period.

Security

Data is stored securely using best practices. We take care to prevent unauthorized access or misuse of any stored user information.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect new features or legal requirements. You will be notified of significant changes through bot announcements or the project website.
