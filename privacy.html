Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2025

Overview

Newsletter Bot respects your privacy. This document outlines what data we collect, how it's used, and your rights regarding that data. This policy applies to all users of the Newsletter Bot on the Discord platform.

Information We Collect

  • Discord User ID: Used to associate your preferences with your account securely.
  • Server ID & Channel IDs: Stored when you configure server feeds or delivery channels.
  • Preferences & Settings: Includes selected news categories, delivery methods (DM or channel), delivery times, formatting style, and keyword scores.
  • Timezone: Manually provided to schedule newsletters appropriately. Not auto-detected.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to deliver customized, scheduled news updates. We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not log or store message content from users.
  • We do not collect email addresses, IP addresses, device info, or location data.
  • We do not store ephemeral interaction responses or slash command results beyond the runtime context.
  • We do not integrate any third-party analytics tools.

Data Retention

User preferences and configuration data are retained only while your account is active. If you remove the bot from your server, your data is automatically deleted.

Data Access and Sharing

Access to user data is strictly limited to the developer and is used solely for debugging and service maintenance. Data is never shared, sold, or transferred to third parties.

Security

All stored data is protected using modern security practices, including credential-based access control and encrypted storage (where applicable). We monitor for unauthorized access and apply patches regularly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw your data by removing the bot or issuing a reset command

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the bot evolves. Major changes will be announced via our Discord support server or website. Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of any new terms.
