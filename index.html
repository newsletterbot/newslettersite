Frequently Asked Questions

📬 Can I control how often I get news?

Yes! You can choose daily or twice-daily digests and select your preferred delivery time.

🛡️ Is my data safe?

We only store the minimum needed to deliver your newsletter — no IPs, no tracking, no profiling.

🌍 What kind of news sources are used?

We aggregate from over 60 vetted RSS feeds including AP, Reuters, Politico, and regional press.

👥 Does it work across multiple servers?

Yes! The bot allows full multi-server support, with isolated settings per server or channel.

📚 Can I view past newsletters?

You can use `/history` or configure your server to archive posts to a read-only channel.

💡 Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We welcome ideas via GitHub or Discord and actively maintain user feedback lists.
