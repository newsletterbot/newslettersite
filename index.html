Yes! You can choose daily or twice-daily digests and select your preferred delivery time.

We only store the minimum needed to deliver your newsletter — no tracking, no profiling, and no sensitive data from your server or user information.

We aggregate news sources across a variety of sources spanning the left, right, center, and more factual-oriented news sources as well. You can subscribe to individual news categories like U.S. politics, world news, or tech — each with its own custom keyword scoring to deliver the most relevant articles.
